About this project

The fintech industry sparked curiosity within us, and thanks to the two-month mentorship the team at Nufi led by Hans Villa and Ilich Núñez gave us, we understood the need to automate the background check, which is an important but time-consuming step in the process of businesses establishing a commercial relationship, currently done manually. Nevertheless, this step is essential for a business to make sure their business partner's legality and financial integrity, and to comply with mandatory state and international laws preventing money laundering and terrorist financing.
